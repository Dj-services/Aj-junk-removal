Our Story

Work & Trust

AJ Junk Removal & Demolition Services LLC was founded with one simple mission: to make junk removal and demolition easy, stress-free, and affordable for every customer we serve.

We proudly serve homeowners, landlords, contractors, and businesses across Massachusetts and New Hampshire. Whether it's a single piece of furniture or a full property cleanout, we treat every job with the same level of care and professionalism.

We show up on time, work efficiently, and leave your space cleaner than we found it. Customer satisfaction isn't just a goal — it's our standard.
